Indian Peafowl

To easily differentiate between the male (Peacock) and female (Peahens), it is noticable that the female has a clorful crest crown sitting on the center of her head and the tails aren't as nearly long as the males.

  • Habitat:Grassland, Forest
  • Life Span: 20 Years
  • Diet: Omnivore
  • Male and Female Names: Peacocks and Peahens

With it's beautiful, exotic feathers that cover the 5 feet longe tail, the Indian Peafowl ranges in the color scheme of shades of blues and greens with hints of orange and yellow. It is said that the feathers grow from the back and extend three times longer than its actual body. How Cool!!!!
